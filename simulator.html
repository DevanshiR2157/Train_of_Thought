Adaptive Trolley Problem Simulator

Experience AI-powered moral dilemmas that adapt to YOUR choices and challenge your ethical framework.

How It Works:

  • Upload the trolley.csv dataset (5000+ real responses)
  • Each scenario adapts based on your previous choices
  • If you prefer saving younger people, the next question removes age as a factor
  • Compare your moral patterns with thousands of real participants
  • No API key needed - runs completely in your browser

Note: Make sure you have the trolley.csv file ready to upload. The simulator uses advanced pattern recognition to create progressively challenging scenarios.
